What is Settle v1.2.1

  • A micro payment app
  • Use between friends and colleagues
  • Support wide range of mobile devices
  • Create groups/pools for lunch, poker, mahjong and travel... etc.
  • Never settle with Cash

How to use

Pay by QRCode
  1. Generate QRCode
  2. Let your friend scan
  3. Check your Balance
Pay on Cloud
  1. Select a friend Or group
  2. Enter amount and send
  3. Your friend will receive a notification

When to use

Meals - Easily split the bill without any mess of loose change or coins. Say Bye to hard to use online banking.

Trip - Track your spending in foreign currency. Settle will do the math for you.

Card Games - Keep track of your winning. Use it as a leader board.
